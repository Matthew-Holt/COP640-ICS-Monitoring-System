About the ICS Monitoring System

This site is designed to provide comprehensive monitoring and management for Industrial Control Systems. Here's what you can do on each page:

Index

The landing page of our website. Here, visitors are greeted and directed to either log in or register to access the ICS monitoring system.

Register

New users can create an account on this page to gain access to the ICS monitoring system, with the option to register as an administrator for full system control.

Login

Users can securely log into their accounts on this page to access personalized system insights and capabilities based on their user role.

Usernames and passwords are stored in a SQLite database. The passwords are encrypted for added security.

Home (Dashboard)

The dashboard is your central hub for system status and alerts. Here, you'll find an overview of your ICS's current state, including any critical notifications.

ICS Environmental Results

The ICS page offers real-time monitoring of environmental results such as temperature and humidity. It also displays manual submissions by administrative users for historical data comparison.

Cloud Services

Discover our cloud-based solutions on this page, designed to enhance your ICS monitoring through additional data storage, backup, and real-time analysis capabilities.

This page lists additional cloud services that may be offered t ICS monitoring system customers.

Admin Panel

Accessible only to administrators, this section allows for the manual input of environmental variables, such as temperature and humidity readings, into the system.
